Meet Jerry

Jerry is a brown anthropomorphic house mouse, who first appeared in the 1940 MGM animated short Puss Gets the Boot. Originally, Hanna gave the mouse's name as Jinx, while Barbera claimed the mouse went unnamed in his first appearance. Regardless, beginning from his next appearance in The Midnight Snack and onwards Jerry was used.

Meet Tom

Tom is a bluish-grey and white anthropomorphic domestic short haired tuxedo British cat who first appeared in the 1940 MGM animated short Puss Gets the Boot. The cat was known as Jasper during his debut in the short; however, beginning with his next appearance in The Midnight Snack he was known as Tom or Thomas.
